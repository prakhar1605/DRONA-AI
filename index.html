AI-Powered Mock Interviews that Adapt to You in Real Time

Drona AI is an LLM-driven mock interview platform that simulates realistic, topic-wise interviews for software, AI/ML, and data roles. The system adapts its questions based on your answers, just like a real interviewer.

Upload your resume or PDFs, answer questions in an interactive Streamlit interface, and get structured feedback on your strengths, weaknesses, and areas to improve — all powered by Python, LangChain, RAG, and modern embedding models.
